Generate sequence

Flags:
+----+---------+-----------------+
| -w | integer | length of output|
+----+---------+-----------------+

Examples:

./sequence 34 53 15
+++++++++
34 53 15
34 15 53
53 34 15
53 15 34
15 34 53
15 53 34
+++++++++

./sequence -w 2 34 53 15
++++++
34 53 
34 15 
53 34 
53 15 
15 34 
15 53 
++++++
